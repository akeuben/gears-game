1

BOOK

Book an appointment with Student Accessibility Services (SAS) via email.

2

CHECK

Double check SAS's site forrequirements for registration.

3

COLLECT

Collect information, forms and documentation required for registration.
Generally, we need at least these documents to begin the process:

  • Diagnosis documentation that covers:
    • The diagnosis made by a licensed medical professional
    • An explanation of how the diagnosis affects your learning
    • Recommendations for accommodations
  • A completed intake form

4

INTAKE

Attend your intake appointment with SAS.
Discuss:

  • Your experiences
  • Barriers
  • Accomodation options

Determine which you would like to try, and what the next steps would be.

5

ASSESS

Monitor how the new accommodations are helping your education experience. If there are different strategies you would like to try or would like to change your current accommodations, book an appointment with your SAS rep.
